01 — Why CTA stays pinned

"Message" docks to the bottom of the screen rather than scrolling away with the bio — the one action this whole page exists to drive is never more than a thumb-reach away.

02 — Why stats sit above the fold

Views, projects, and rating appear before any portfolio image does — a brand deciding whether to hire someone scans credibility first, work samples second.

03 — Why the card is shared

Building one portfolio card component now — reused unchanged in Day 04's directory grid — means no second design pass and no visual drift between the two screens.
