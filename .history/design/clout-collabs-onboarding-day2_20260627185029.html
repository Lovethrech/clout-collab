01 — Why Google-first

Most creators are already signed into Google on their phone, leading with it cuts signup friction before they ever see a password field.

02 — Why role comes after

Role selection is its own screen after account creation, not bundled into signup, one decision at a time keeps first-screen drop-off low.

03 — Why the gradient appears once

The signature gradient is held back until this final "Continue", it marks the one moment that finishes onboarding, not just another button.
