01 — Why a fork, not a form

This screen asks zero questions — just a choice. Anyone arriving for the first time gets oriented before they're asked for an email or password.

02 — Why Create Account leads

As the primary gradient button, it's the assumed default for a marketplace still building its base — new signups are the priority action, with Log In as the clear secondary path.

03 — Why Google/email choice waits

Auth method selection happens one screen later, on the dedicated Sign Up or Log In screen — keeping this fork to a single decision.
